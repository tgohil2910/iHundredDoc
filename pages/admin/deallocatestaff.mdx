# De-Allocate Staff

Upon selecting the De-Allocate Staff option from the Menu in admin dashboard, This Page will open up.

![De-Allocate Staff](src/assets/admin/deallocatestaff.png)

First, Select a Staff from the Dropdown,

![Select Staff](src/assets/admin/selectstaff-de.png)

Once the staff is selected, the list of the students who have been assigned to a selected verifier will be displayed.

![Students View](src/assets/admin/studentsview-de.png)

Now, Select the students and click on the Release button.

![Release Students](src/assets/admin/release.png)
