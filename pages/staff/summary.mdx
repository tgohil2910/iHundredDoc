# Summary

Upon Clicking on the Summary from the Navbar in staff dashboard, This Page will open up.

![Summary](src/assets/staff/summary.png)

This Summary Page displays the number of students allocated to the staff, activities uploaded by the students, activities
approved by the staff, activities pending to be verified and activities rejected by the staff.
