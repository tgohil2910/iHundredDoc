# Allocate Staff

Upon selecting the Allocate Staff option from the Menu in admin dashboard, This Page will open up.

![Allocate Staff](src/assets/admin/allocatestaff.png)

First, Select a Staff from the Dropdown,

![Select Staff](src/assets/admin/selectstaff.png)

Then, Select a batch of the students

![Select Batch](src/assets/admin/selectbatch.png)

Once the batch is selected, the list of the students who have not been assigned a verifier will be displayed.

![Students View](src/assets/admin/studentsview.png)

Now, Select the students and click on the Assign button.

![Allocate Students](src/assets/admin/allocate.png)
