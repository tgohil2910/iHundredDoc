# Signup/Signin

To Signup or Signin, Click on the **login** button available on top right corner.

![landing page](src/assets/login.png)

This will open a Signup/Signin Modal dialog.

![Signup/Signin Modal](src/assets/google-toast.png)

If you are looking at this screen for the first time then click on the **Signup with Google** or If you have already performed Signup before then Click on **login**. In either case you
will be redirected to the google login page.

![Google Login Page](src/assets/google-login-page.png)

After Successful authentication with google, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

![Student dashboard](src/assets/dummy-student-dashboard.png)
